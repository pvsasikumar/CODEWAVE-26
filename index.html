About the Event

CodeWave'26 is a hackathon organized by the Department of Computer Applications, SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Ramapuram. This event brings together creative and passionate minds to tackle real-world challenges through innovative technological solutions.

Whether you're interested in web development, mobile applications, artificial intelligence, or smart campus innovations, CodeWave offers the perfect platform to showcase your skills and ideas. The event is open to students from all colleges, encouraging diverse participation, collaboration, and the exchange of knowledge across institutions.

This hackathon focuses on innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving, challenging participants to ideate, design, develop, and present impactful solutions. It's not just about writing code‚Äîit's about transforming ideas into meaningful, real-world applications.

Rules & Guidelines

01

Team Composition

Teams must consist of 2‚Äì4 members. Solo participation is not allowed. Choose your teammates wisely for the best collaboration.

02

Cross-College Teams

Participants may form teams with students from different colleges. This encourages diverse perspectives and broader networking opportunities.

03

Internet Access

Internet usage is permitted during the hackathon. You may use online resources, documentation, and APIs to enhance your project.

04

Pre-built Templates

Using pre-built templates, frameworks, and libraries is allowed. However, the core logic and functionality must be developed during the event.

05

Plagiarism Policy

Any form of plagiarism or copying existing projects will lead to immediate disqualification. Originality is key to winning.

06

Submission Rules

Each team is allowed only one final submission. Ensure your project is complete and tested before the submission deadline.
